Frequently Asked Questions

Unsure About Something?

We may already have your answer!

We aim to keep our business model simple, straight-forward, and transparent - but that doesn't mean you don't have questions about how things work! Check below to see some answers to the most common questions we come across, and if you can't find what you are looking for, be sure to reach out to us!

Reach Out To Us
What does the process for getting a website made look like for me?
When you decide that you are ready for a proper digital presence, head down to our contact form and reach out to us! We'll begin by sending over a quick form to collect some important initial information such as what packages you might be interested in, various aspects of your business, and generally what vibe you want your site to embody. We will then design your site for you, requiring no time investment on your end. We review our ideas with you, and get to work developing it once you approve our design.
How long does a website take to make?
Typical build cycles average at roughly between 2 and 4 weeks from initiation to the final go-live publish. Simple one-to-three page brochure sites generally fall accurately on the 2 weeks marker (or shorter in some cases!), requiring very little time input from you as the site-builder. If you are wanting a highly customized, large scale operation, timeframes can sometimes scale beyond 4 weeks. Most of our sites however lie somewhere safely in between 2-4 weeks.
How do I make edits to my website once it is finished?
If you want or need an edit to the content on your site, you just simply need to send us an email specifying the change! Because all of our contracts natively include maintenance, hosting, updates, edits, etc. - you can submit change requests up to three times a calendar week without incurring any additional charges. To make our $0 down monthly pricing possible, we are constantly working in order to support and update your site along with our own active ecosystem - we do not offer login capabilities for businesses to act as their own site-administrator to change their own content ad-hoc. We do offer CMS capabilities to standard Up-Front sites through Netlify + Decap at an additional $25/mo maintenance fee, and Shopify integrations.
Who is the Up Front pricing plan for?
By opting into an Up-Front pricing plan, you skip the mandatory required 18 month contract period of a monthly tier. You pay entirely for the website on day 1 (minus a required $25/mo maintenance fee), and do not end up paying the 'financed' price inflation that an 18-month agreement naturally comes with.
What happens after I pay?
When an Up-Front site is delivered to you, you immediately take complete and total ownership of the website! Provided it is paid in full, we hold absolutely no copyright/legal bindings over the project whatsoever, nor does the site belong in part with us - it belongs completely to you and your business.
Will my Up Front tier plan include SEO too?
While Monthly tier subscriptions come with SEO monitoring and strategy formulation organically, Up Front packages do necessitate a secondary, separate 'Premium On-Going SEO' package (at $100/mo) in order to get the high quality, on-going organic Google growth you'd see paired with a Monthly. However all tier-plans - including Up-Fronts - are shipped from $0 built fundamentally with 100/100 Core Web Vitals to lay critical foundations!
Is my Monthly plan a contract?
Yes, for our standard Monthly tier ($200/mo), you are required to hold onto your contract for 18 calendar months after signing. Since our monthly sites carry a $0 down build cost, these monthly contracts act as the finance period to cover both the $0 down inception phase of development as well as standard support and maintenance moving forward!
Can I buy out my Monthly Plan early?
There are many situations where purchasing your site outright at the Up Front rate makes more sense. Because of that, you can buy out of your Monthly contract. Your remaining buyout cost is determined by whatever the 'Up Front' price for your website was, minus whatever amount your prior months' payments already covered. Please be aware that if you buy out your plan, but still want us to host/manage it, you will have to assume the standard $25/mo maintenance fee.
Who is the Essentials plan for?
The Essentials package was made for businesses that require only the bare minimum digital presence with the lowest cost impact. Specifically for those that desire a modern quality that outpaces off-the-shelf templates, but do not have the need to scale their site out further than a few pages or support complicated workflows.
Can I upgrade out of Essentials plan?
Absolutely! If your business continues to grow and you begin to find your need for more complex internal scaling and development increases, you can choose to upgrade your agreement to our other standard Monthly tier, unlocking the benefits enclosed within them.
Will I own my Domain?
Absolutely again! The domain (the text that follows 'https://' in your address bar!) is considered separate from the website's code itself. Whether we acquire and pay the yearly-renewed registry fees for a domain for you, or you acquire it yourself, the domain remains your property unequivocally and we will transfer ownership to you immediately should you choose to move providers.
Do you accept Credit Cards?
We accept Credit Card, Google Pay, and Apple Pay payments for Monthly costs, handled securely through Stripe. However, large Up-Front build costs must be done via direct ACH or Bank Transfer.
How late can I go without paying?
Invoices must be paid upon established delivery timeframes or standard intervals. Standard intervals hold a 7 day grace period where late fees will then begin to be accrued. If an invoice remains unpaid past designated dates outlined in your contract, we reserve the right to temporarily drop your active deployment from our servers until payments are resolved.
