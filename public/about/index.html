About Us

Websites that are
Coded For You

Hello there, my name is Nick - I am the owner of Good For You Web Services. In a past life, I worked in video game development. One day, the project I had been working on was suddenly canceled by the studio, and most of my coworkers laid off - I decided it was time to leave the world of game development behind. Prior to leaving the studio, I had found myself developing web interfaces for some internal tools to streamline certain processes for other developers. When I left, I decided that I wanted to pursue this line of work full time. So I spent a year taking courses to fill the gaps in my knowledge, then began to build my own business!

The vision for Good For You Web Services is to cater to the needs of other small businesses on the world wide web. Developing a presence online is a difficult task for small businesses owners. Many individuals turn to popular website building services, only to find they require vast amounts of time (they don't have) and money (better spent elsewhere) to end up with a sub-par experience that doesn't feel unique. Often times businesses will contract a web developer, but are faced with having to pay large down payments and fees that just aren't in the books. Even then, many businesses are lucky to get a product worth what they paid, and have no available ongoing support for their website.

This is a difficult spot to be in for many businesses, and the outcome is often a very poor website, or no website at all. I started Good For You Web Services to make high quality, custom websites within reach of any small business. We do this by offering a $0 down pricing plan - for a low monthly fee your business can have a bespoke online presence developed by a fellow small business. We are reputable and accountable, and we will make something you'll love or your money back!

How We Work

We handle it all, start to finish.

Our development process is designed to give you the best results while being minimally demanding of your time. We collect a good amount of information up front, and input we require from you can always be provided at your schedule's convenience. Our process generally takes between 2-4 weeks from start to finish, depending on various factors.

Step I
Initial Information

When you reach out to us, we will send you a form to collect some initial information about you/your business to help us get an idea of what you want.

Step II
Design

Once we have that information, we take care of the rest! First, we get to work designing the visuals, appearance, and identity of your website.

Step III
Approval

Once the design of your website reaches a satisfactory state, we will meet with you to go over it. We continue to make tweaks and edits until you are satisfied and we receive approval from you.

Step IV
Development

With your approval, we begin working on the functionality of your website. At various stages of development we provide you with links to WIP demos.

Step V
Content

At this point, we will work with you to replace all placeholder content from development with meaningful content that characterizes your business.

Step VI
Publishing

When your website is finished and passes your final approval, we can publish it and your brand new, custom website will be available for anyone to visit!
